Meet Nicole

Your Central New Jersey advocate, neighbor, and negotiator.

I am Nicole Slohoda, a Realtor-Associate with Keller Williams Elite, Realtors in Metuchen. I have built my practice on one belief: real estate is personal, and clients deserve someone who treats it that way — not a transaction machine, not a part-timer, and not someone who disappears after the contract is signed.

I work across Middlesex County, Union County, Woodbridge Township, and the surrounding towns. Whether you are buying your first home, moving up, downsizing, relocating into New Jersey, or selling a family property — I will walk you through every step with straight answers and a steady hand.
